CESS - Computer Engineering Student Society

March 10 at 1:45 PM ·

DAY 1
A remarkable milestone for innovation and progress in Dagupan City!

As part of the dynamic lineup of activities spearheaded by the Department of Science and Technology (DOST) across the city, one of the most meaningful highlights was held at Universidad de Dagupan—the Ribbon Cutting and Signing Ceremony of the IHUB / Innovation Hub.

This significant event marks more than just the opening of a new facility; it represents a powerful commitment to nurturing creativity, research, entrepreneurship, and technological advancement within the region. The IHUB stands as a collaborative space where students, faculty, innovators, and industry partners can transform bold ideas into real-world solutions that address community needs and global challenges.

Through this initiative, DOST and Universidad de Dagupan strengthen their shared vision of building a future driven by science, technology, and innovation. The ceremony symbolizes unity, partnership, and a renewed dedication to empowering the next generation of engineers, researchers, and change-makers.

With the doors of the Innovation Hub now open, Dagupan City takes a confident step forward—fostering breakthroughs, cultivating talent, and inspiring a culture of innovation that will shape a smarter, more resilient tomorrow. 🚀

CESS - Computer Engineering Student Society

March 11 at 10:30 AM ·

DAY 2!!!
DOST Robotics excellence unleashed at CSI Stadia! 🤖🔥

Our Computer Engineering students proudly stepped into the RoboCup arena to compete in the highly challenging BIN COLLECTION category, bringing with them not just one, but two innovative robots engineered from the ground up. Each machine was the result of countless hours of designing, programming, assembling, and rigorous testing—showcasing their technical expertise in robotics, embedded systems, automation, and intelligent control.

On the competition floor, precision met strategy as the robots navigated obstacles, executed tasks, and demonstrated the power of teamwork and problem-solving under pressure. More than just a contest, this experience reflected the students’ passion for innovation and their commitment to transforming knowledge into real-world application.

From concept to competition, they proved that true engineering is built on dedication, creativity, and the courage to compete. 🚀✨
